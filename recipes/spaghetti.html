Sapghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish made with long, thin noodles that are boiled to a perfect al dente texture and often served with a rich and flavorful sauce. The most popular variation, spaghetti with marinara, features a tomato-based sauce simmered with garlic, onions, and aromatic herbs like basil and oregano. Ground meats, such as beef or pork, are often added to create a hearty Bolognese, while simple additions like olive oil, fresh parsley, and parmesan cheese make for a lighter dish.

Spaghetti can be paired with a variety of toppings and ingredients to suit different tastes. Meatballs, seafood, or roasted vegetables can transform the dish into a gourmet meal. Typically garnished with freshly grated parmesan and a sprinkle of black pepper, it is a versatile recipe that balances texture and flavor. Served with a side of garlic bread or a crisp green salad, spaghetti is a beloved comfort food enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add a generous pinch of salt to the boiling water (about 1 tsp per liter).
  3. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions (usually 8-10 minutes) until al dente.
  4. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti and set it aside.
  5. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or saucepan over medium heat.
  6. Add the minced garlic and chopped onions, and sauté until softened and fragrant (about 2-3 minutes).
  7. Stir in the tomato paste (if using) and cook for 1 minute to deepen the flavor.
  8. Add the crushed or diced tomatoes, dried basil, and oregano. Stir well to combine.
  9. Season with salt, pepper, and sugar (if desired, to reduce acidity).
  10. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. If the sauce is too thick, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to loosen it.
  11. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  12. Simmer for 1-2 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  13. Serve hot, garnished with grated parmesan cheese and fresh basil or parsley.
